Introduction

History

The Castle family has owned the Cheboygan home along the shores of the Straits of Mackinac since the late 1940's when, shortly after World War II, Thomas Frost bought the double lot located between Cheboygan and Mackinaw City. The lot and the house have changed hands but always remained in the family.

Future

In 2020, Gerry Castle's surviving siblings decided to invest funds from his estate into re-modeling the Cheboygan house. After purchasing the house and property from his parent's estate, Gerry had expressed his plans, on several occasions, to fix up the Cheboygan house and make it a place to where our extended family could return. In honor of his wishes and in memory of Gerry and other family members who are no longer with us, the house will be renovated for the enjoyment of future generations.

House Re-model Vision

Re-model Project Scope

The scope of the project is to re-model every room in the house - only the garage and attic will be left undisturbed. Additionally, a large deck will be extended off the front of the house facing Lake Huron. New plantings will be landscaped around the house, and saplings will be planted throughout the property replacing the many trees that have fallen over the last decade.

Re-model Goals and Principles

Home improvements will be undertaken with a goal of simplifying and extending the maintainability of the home. A guiding principle of the renovations is to retain the house and property's charm and comfort so that it would be recognizable to previous vistors and occupants. However, the house is a living space and not a museum. Future projects will make the house more attractive and comfortable as a vacation getaway for our next generations.

House Remodel Overview

Multi-phased Project

Renovation and re-modeling work will be broken into distinct phases spanning multiple years. Construction will bein in the late fall and complete by early spring, so that the entire house is available for use and enjoyment during the late-spring, summer, and peak-fall color seasons. Beginning in the fall of 2022, re-modeling projects will be structured so that the house can take visitors during winter weekends while construction is isolated to specific rooms, and any power and water outages can be scheduled during the week.
